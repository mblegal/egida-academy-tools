# Course journal

A formal document for running a vocational course. This template is ready to print and fill in. Fields marked with an ellipsis `.............` and empty table cells are completed by the instructor or the coordinator. Rows marked with the word **EXAMPLE** serve only as a model for filling in and should be deleted or overwritten with real data.

> Document glossary (key terms, with the Polish original):
> - **Class log (PL: Dziennik zajec)** - a chronological record of each day of the course (topic, hours, format, instructor).
> - **Attendance register (PL: Rejestr obecnosci)** - a table for marking who was present on a given day.
> - **Workstation instruction (PL: Instruktaz stanowiskowy)** - health and safety training for a specific workstation, given before work at that workstation begins.
> - **PPE (PL: SOI, srodki ochrony indywidualnej)** - personal protective equipment (helmet, protective footwear, high-visibility vest, gloves, goggles, hearing protectors).
> - **Attendance rate (PL: Frekwencja)** - a participant's attendance counted as a percentage of course days.
> - **Certificate (PL: Zaswiadczenie)** - the final document confirming completion of the course.
